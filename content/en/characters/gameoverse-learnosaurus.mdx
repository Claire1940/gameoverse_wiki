<Section icon={Shield} title="Navigating Gameoverse Rules in Gobbles' World">
Operating within _Gobbles & the Learnosaurus_ requires a keen understanding of the overarching rules of the Gameoverse. These rules dictate character interactions, the consequences of completing games, and the limitations faced by external agents.

### Gameoverse Critical Rules

| Rule Name | Description | Impact on Gameplay |
|---|---|---|
| **Win Condition** | Hero defeats villain | World self-destructs, turns to Float |
| **Static** | External agents reveal Gameoverse rules to natives | Game world freezes, foreign elements scanned for deletion |
| **Security Lock** | Prevents foreign characters from harming natives | Ensures game integrity, can be broken post-destruction |
| **Game Over** | Character dies outside their game | Permanent death, converts to Float |

### Preventing World Destruction

Farcade agents, like Kai and Gabbu (the Learnosaurus), are tasked with preventing game worlds from reaching their Win Condition. This often involves intricate strategies to disrupt the hero's progress without triggering "Static" or other adverse effects.

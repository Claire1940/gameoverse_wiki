<Section icon={Map} title="The Ambiguity of Heroism and Villainy in Gameoverse">
A compelling theory surrounding *Gameoverse* suggests that the traditional roles of heroes and villains might be inverted. Warrick, despite his destructive methods, aims to "change the rules" and restore what was lost, which could be seen as a heroic endeavor from a certain perspective. Conversely, Kit and the Farcade, in their attempts to *prevent* world destruction by helping villains, might inadvertently be hindering Warrick's larger goal of universal restoration.

<Callout type="tip">
Consider the possibility that Warrick's actions, while seemingly villainous, are a desperate attempt to create a better Gameoverse, making him a tragic figure rather than purely evil. This moral ambiguity is a core theme.
</Callout>

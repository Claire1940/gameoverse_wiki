<Section icon={Swords} title="Kit's Unique Role in the Gameoverse">
Unlike traditional heroes who defeat villains, Kit's objective is to ensure the villains *don't* lose. This is a direct consequence of the *Gameoverse* premise: if a game's hero successfully defeats the villain, the entire game world is annihilated. This twisted rule forces Kit to rethink what it means to be a savior.

### Appearance and Design

Snappers is depicted as a massive shark, instantly recognizable by his floating yellow crown. His physique is bulky with a teardrop silhouette, featuring a dark grey upper body and a lighter underbelly. Notable physical traits include short, sharp horns, a set of menacing teeth with larger bottom canines, a light pink tongue, and a partially ripped dorsal and caudal fin. He consistently wears a grumpy scowl, reinforcing his villainous persona. During training sessions with Kaboodle, he sports a blue boxing helmet and gloves, both adorned with yellow crown markings.

### Core Abilities

- **Fire Breathing**: Snappers can breathe fire and expel solid fireballs. He controls their size, saving the largest for last-resort attacks. These fireballs can be knocked back.
- **Lava Immunity**: He is completely unharmed by direct contact with lava, although this does not extend to general fire immunity.
- **Supernatural Roar**: A powerful bellow that creates shockwaves, capable of physically altering the environment, such as breaking stalactites.

The fundamental concept of **Gameoverse lore** revolves around video game worlds that are horrifically exploding. The only way to prevent this ultimate destruction is for characters to actively help the villains stop the good guys. This counter-intuitive premise stems from the idea that once a "good guy" wins and beats the game, the game itself comes to an end, leading to the world's deletion. The series title, "Game Overse," cleverly plays on "Game Overs," hinting at these catastrophic conclusions.

<Callout type="tip" title="From Concept to Reality">
The **Gameoverse reboot** evolved over 11 years, starting development in 2015. Creator Ross O'Donovan, also known as RubberRoss, collaborated with Arin Hanson to bring this vision to life. After numerous rejections from studios, Hanson self-financed the project, eventually catching the attention of Glitch Productions, known for its commitment to indie animation.

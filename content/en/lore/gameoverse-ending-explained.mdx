Farcaid, founded by Dusk, is comprised of heroes who, like Kit, inadvertently caused the destruction of their own worlds by fulfilling their heroic duties. Their mission is to intervene in other worlds, stopping heroes from defeating their villains and thereby preventing planetary annihilation. They believe that keeping games in a perpetual, unfinished state is the only way to preserve life.

Syntax, on the other hand, is a villainous group that actively aids heroes in completing their games, accelerating world destruction. Their leader, Warwick, aims to harvest the energy (called "Float") from these destroyed planets to gather enough power to rewrite the programming of the entire Gameoverse. Their ultimate goal is to change the rules so that game completion no longer leads to destruction, hoping to bring back everything that was lost.

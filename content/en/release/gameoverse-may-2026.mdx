<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Gameoverse Mechanics in May 2026">

The *Gameoverse* operates on a fundamental principle: each game world contains a hero and a villain. The completion of a game, specifically the hero defeating the villain, triggers the world's destruction, turning it into "Float Crystals." This critical mechanic drives the actions of the main characters, Kai and Gabb, who are tasked with preventing such outcomes. Their mission is to identify heroes and villains and report back to Farkaede, an organization seemingly dedicated to preserving these worlds, albeit through unconventional means.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Lore: World Destruction">
If a hero successfully defeats their villain within a game world, that world will **explode**, yielding valuable Float Crystals but erasing its existence. This is the central conflict the protagonists aim to prevent.
</Callout>
